About

What an incredible opportunity it is to merge my passions in life - learning, technology, connection and Mindfulness.

I have been immersed in the teachings and practice of Mindfulness since 2011 and have seen phenomenal changes in my life.

In March 2015 I began a course to become a web developer and this site is my first offering, fully coded and built from scratch. The gallery itself is a sharing of some of my most treasured musings and experiences.

I live an inspired life and love to create. I am thrilled at the prospect of boundless possibilities to explore as a consequence of these new skills.

To see more of my crazy yoga shenanigans and other antics check out the Raw Yoga Facebook Page or follow me on twitter @carolyncarama
